# 📦 BUYER'S START GUIDE

Welcome to the **Academic AI Automation Kit**! 🎓

You now have the power to process academic literature at 10x speed. 

### 🚀 How to get started right now:
If you are not a coder, don't worry! 

1. Open the **Google Colab Notebook** included in this package.
2. Click "Run All" (Runtime $\rightarrow$ Run all).
3. Click the link that appears at the bottom to open your AI Dashboard.
4. Paste your text and get your summary!

### 📂 What's in this folder:
- `INSTALL_GUIDE.md`: How to set this up on your own computer.
- `USER_GUIDE.md`: Tips on how to get the best summaries.
- `PROMPTS.md`: Advanced prompts to use with LLMs.
- `EXAMPLES.md`: See what a "perfect" output looks like.
