# ⚡ POWER PROMPTS FOR RESEARCH

While the toolkit automates the summary, you can use these prompts in ChatGPT/Claude to further synthesize the results:

**The Critique Prompt:**
*"I have a summary of a paper. Please identify 3 potential biases in the methodology and suggest 2 ways the authors could have improved the sample size."*

**The Gap Finder:**
*"Compare these two summaries. What is the fundamental disagreement between Paper A and Paper B, and how can I bridge this gap in my own research?"*
